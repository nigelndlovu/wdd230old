Target Audience

  • Who: Businesses within the city and accross the country seeking investment opportunities and participation in the business events held througout the year hosted by the chamber.
  • Age: 21 to 70 years
  • Income: Annual revenue of above $30,000.00.
  • Device compatibility: The website should be easily accessible in handheld devices like smartphone and tablets. Also accessible in destops or laptops of any sizes.
  • Education: Post High School education
  • Experience: Is user friendly and easily accesible from small vendors to large corporates.
